## Staking Economics (Why It Works)

### Can You Actually Afford 50% APY?

```
Simple Math:

Customer stakes 1,000 CT02
Rate: 50% APY
Yearly reward needed: 500 CT02

Where does this 500 CT02 come from?
├─ 10% of monthly minted tokens go to reward pool
├─ If 35,500 tokens minted in Year 1
├─ Reward pool: 3,550 CT02
├─ Average staked tokens: ~15,000 (40% participation)
├─ APY on staked = 3,550 / 15,000 = 23.7%

Wait - that's only 23.7%, not 50%!

But here's why it's sustainable:
1. Early Year 1: Few stakers, can offer 50% (low volume)
2. Mid Year 1: More stakers, reduce to 35% (built-in escape hatch)
3. Late Year 1: Profitable operations, can afford 25-30% permanently
4. Year 2+: Additional revenue streams (API, subs) fund rewards

Strategy: Front-load high APY to incentivize early adoption, then normalize
```
